100 Burfords — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

100 Burfords is an unofficial fan tribute to the Burford cartoon series by Gary B. Martin, published weekly in the West Side Rag.

Data We Collect

100 Burfords does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app contains no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no user accounts.

Cartoon Content

All cartoon images and article content are bundled within the app or fetched from the West Side Rag's public website. No user data is involved in this process.

Reader Comments

Comments displayed in the app are public comments sourced from the West Side Rag website. The app does not store or transmit your identity when you view them. Tapping "Add Your Comment" opens the West Side Rag website in Safari; any data you submit there is governed by the West Side Rag's own privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services.
